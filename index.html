The Second Death of Daedalus Mole

Coming soon from Unbound

A haunting, darkly comic space opera set in a post-Earth universe,
in which human beings are rare and human relationships are sparse.

The Second Death of Daedalus Mole

Daedalus Mole is a courier, an alcoholic and one of the last few thousand humans alive. He's also broke.

When his day off becomes an opportunity to make some fast cash, he finds himself dragged into a game of interstellar cat-and-mouse, where the cats are everywhere and the mouse is a high-strung fugitive.
